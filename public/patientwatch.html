PatientWatch

Help is just a click away

Our Product

PatientWatch improves your patients experience in just 3 easy steps

Request

Patients are given a pager that lets them request assistance from their nurse.

Transmit

The request is then transmitted to the patients nurse via the PatientWatch.

Receive

The nurse then recieves the page on their PatientWatch and responds to the request as soon as they can.
